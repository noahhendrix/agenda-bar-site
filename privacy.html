Agenda Bar

Privacy Policy

Look, let’s keep this simple. Agenda Bar is a calendar app. While we do require access to your calendar data, we access that through secure and trusted APIs provided by Apple. We don’t transmit that data to our servers or third-party services other than what’s provided by Apple.

We access Calendar data through Apple’s EventKit framework. For events with location data we render a map of the event’s location using Apple’s secure and private MapKit framework.

We do not track your usage or collect any personal information about you.

If there are changes to this policy we will post them on this page. If you have questions, please email support@agendabar.app.
